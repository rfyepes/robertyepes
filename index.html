About

Hi, I'm Robert. I'm currently working on my undergraduate degree at Tufts University, where I'm majoring in Computer Science and Cognitive & Brain Science. I'm interested in a wide range of fields, from artificial intelligence to cybersecurity - but ever since taking a class on web programming (which I've now become a TA for), I've become most passionate about all aspects of web development - both front-end and back-end. I heavily value collaboration, and hope to participate in an environment that reflects these values.
